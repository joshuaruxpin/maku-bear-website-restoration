homepage > About Maku.com

  maku.com is a social network platform for parents of infants and toddlers aged 0-6 years old under the umbrella of Shanghai Xitao Trespass Digital Technology Company. Users can share audio and video works that help the growth and education of infants and toddlers, record their children's growth experiences, share their pregnancy and parenting experiences, and discuss life topics. At the same time, Multi-Channel provides users with accurate delivery of information, so that users can actively, quickly, easily and happily enjoy the services provided by Mako.com. We firmly believe that parents can harvest happiness, precipitate happiness, record love, and display love in the happiest family of Maku.com.


  maku.com provides four main services for users: maku bears, maku chat, focus topics, and activity zones.


  maku Bear is a resource sharing place for young parents. It is also a place where young parents can freely play and create new products.


  Focus Topic is a communication platform for young parents. She focuses on the exchange of parenting experience and family life, and facilitates users to communicate with young parents of the same city, age and interest.


  maku chat is a baby's online growing up album, a small world to record baby's childish words and show off baby's photo talents.


  From time to time, the Activity Zone will post all kinds of member activities organized by Mako.com as well as parent-child bonding activities organized by Maku members.
